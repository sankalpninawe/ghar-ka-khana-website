About Us

For those who find themselves far from the comforting embrace of their home and the familiar flavors of their kitchen, "Ghar Ka Khana" is your virtual kitchen companion. We understand the nostalgia and yearning for homemade meals that come with living away from home. Our website is your gateway to rekindling the joy of home-cooked food, offering a treasure trove of recipes, culinary tips, and a supportive community of fellow homesick cooks.
Whether you're a student, a professional, or simply someone who craves the warmth of home in every bite, "Ghar Ka Khana" is your culinary haven.
Join us on this delectable journey, as we bring the love and flavors of home straight to your kitchen, no matter where you are in the world. Welcome to the taste of home, away from home!

Ghar Ka Khana is for everyone who believes that the kitchen is at the heart of the home, and food is the center of a life well-lived.

Cauliflower Tikka Masala

Cauliflower Tikka Masala is a mouthwatering dish featuring roasted cauliflower florets smothered in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce, bursting with Indian spices. It's a delightful vegetarian twist on the classic chicken tikka masala.

Masala Aloo

Masala Aloo, a beloved Indian dish, consists of tender, spiced potato cubes that are pan-fried to perfection, delivering a flavorful and comforting side dish or snack. This delectable dish showcases the bold flavors of Indian spices in every bite.

Crispy Kurkuri Bhindi

Crispy Kurkuri Bhindi is a delightful Indian appetizer or side dish featuring thinly sliced okra coated in a spiced gram flour batter, deep-fried until irresistibly crunchy and flavorful, making it a popular and satisfying snack.


Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a classic North Indian dish, with tender cubes of paneer simmered in a creamy tomato-based gravy, enriched with aromatic spices, and a touch of butter, creating a flavorful vegetarian delight.


Matar Paneer

Spicy Dal Tadka

Dal Tadka is a classic Indian lentil dish known for its aromatic tempering of spices like cumin, mustard seeds, and garlic, enhancing the flavor of cooked lentils, resulting in a comforting and flavorful staple in Indian cuisine.


Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, typically filled with a flavorful spiced potato mixture. This crispy and savory delicacy is a beloved breakfast item, celebrated for its unique taste and texture.

Hyderabadi Veg Dum Biryani

Hyderabadi Veg Dum Biryani is an aromatic and flavorful Indian rice dish prepared with basmati rice, assorted vegetables and spices, all slow-cooked together to create a mouthwatering one-pot meal. This royal dish is a testament to the culinary artistry of Hyderabad.

Buttery Pav Bhaji

Buttery Pav Bhaji is a lip-smacking Indian street food dish made from a rich and spicy vegetable curry, served with soft buttered pav. This indulgent combination of flavors and textures is a favorite among food enthusiasts, known for its satisfying and buttery goodness.

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a beloved North Indian dish featuring tender, tandoori-cooked chicken pieces immersed in a creamy, tomato-based gravy enriched with butter. It is famous for its rich, indulgent taste and has a worldwide fanbase for its combination of succulent chicken and velvety sauce.

Chicken 65

Chicken 65 is a popular Indian appetizer, consisting of bite-sized pieces of spicy, deep-fried chicken that are seasoned with a mix of aromatic spices and curry leaves. This tantalizing dish is renowned for its fiery flavor and crispy texture, making it a favorite choice among chicken lovers.

Sheet Pan Tandoori Chicken

Sheet pan tandoori chicken is a delicious dish where marinated chicken pieces are roasted on a baking sheet, capturing the bold flavors of tandoori spices and creating a mouthwatering, fuss-free meal. This recipe offers all the essence of traditional tandoori chicken but with a single pan cooking method.

Chicken Shawarma

Chicken shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food consisting of tender, thinly sliced marinated chicken, served in pita bread or flatbread, and accompanied by a variety of flavorful toppings, sauces, and vegetables. It is one of the favorite dishes in Middle Eastern cuisine.

Mutton Curry

Mutton curry is a hearty and flavorful dish featuring tender pieces of mutton cooked in a rich and aromatic gravy, seasoned with a blend of spices and herbs. This dish is a cherished culinary delight across many cultures, known for its deep, savory taste and versatility in preparation.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken biryani is a popular Indian one-pot dish made with fragrant basmati rice and succulent chicken pieces, cooked together with a blend of aromatic spices and herbs, resulting in a delectable and flavorful meal. This classic dish is celebrated for its exquisite taste and is a symbol of Indian culinary heritage.
